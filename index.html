Chariot

The Chariot arcana is the seventh (VII) tarot arcana and is the first social link you get. The confidant of the Chariot social link is Ryuji Sakamoto


Lovers

The Death arcana is the thirteenth (XIII) tarot arcana and can be unlocked during the first month of the game. The confidant of the Death social link is Tae Takemi


Devil

The Devil arcana is the fifteenth (XV) tarot arcana and can be unlocked towards the end of the first third of the game. The confidant of the Devil social link is Ichiko Ohya


Emperor

The Emperor arcana is the fourth (IV) tarot arcana and can be unlocked after fully completing the third Palace. The confidant of the Emperor social link is Yusuke Kitagawa


Empress

The Empress arcana is the third (III) tarot arcana and can be unlocked after completing the fifth Palace. The confidant of the Empress social link is Haru Okumura
